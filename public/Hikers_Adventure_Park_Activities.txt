HIKERS ADVENTURE PARK - ACTIVITIES

1.  Eagle Flight (Zipline) Climb a wooden ladder, cross a 38-foot
    suspension bridge, and experience an exciting zipline ride with
    panoramic views of the adventure park before landing on the Multi
    Activity Tower.

2.  Sky Cycle Experience the thrill of riding India’s longest and
    highest Sky Cycle in Rishikesh. Ride high above the ground while
    enjoying breathtaking views and an unforgettable adventure.

3.  Sky Cycle (Kids) A kid-friendly version of the Sky Cycle designed to
    provide a safe and exciting elevated cycling experience for young
    adventurers.

4.  Superman Shot Feel the sensation of flying like a superhero as
    you’re launched through the air in one of the park’s signature
    adrenaline-pumping attractions.

5.  Giant Swing Swing through the air with incredible speed and height
    while enjoying a thrilling free-fall sensation.

6.  Reverse Bungee Also known as the Sling Shot, this ride catapults
    participants high into the sky, creating an exhilarating experience.

7.  Rock Climbing Challenge your strength and determination by climbing
    a rock wall with professional safety equipment.

8.  Rock Climbing (50 Feet) Scale a 50-foot climbing wall designed to
    test endurance, balance, and confidence.

9.  Go Kart (Adult) Drive eco-friendly electric go-karts on a specially
    designed racing track.

10. Go Kart (Adult Couple) Enjoy an electric go-kart ride together with
    a companion.

11. Go Kart (Kids) A fun and safe electric go-kart experience designed
    for young racers.

12. Couple Go-Kart A shared racing experience for couples on the
    dedicated circuit.

13. Tandem Sky Cycle Ride a Sky Cycle together with a partner for a
    unique aerial adventure.

14. Tandem Zipline Experience ziplining alongside a partner with scenic
    aerial views.

15. Sky Roller An elevated rolling adventure combining movement,
    balance, and panoramic views.

16. Rappelling Descend a vertical surface using ropes and safety gear
    under expert supervision.

17. Slithering Learn controlled rope descent techniques while navigating
    steep surfaces.

18. Commando Net Climbing Test your agility and upper-body strength on a
    military-style cargo net.

19. Tyre Wall Climbing Climb a wall made of interconnected tyres that
    challenges coordination and grip strength.

20. Rope Course Navigate more than 30 rope obstacles designed to test
    physical ability and mental focus.

21. Multi Activities Tower & Rope Course A combination of climbing
    elements, rope obstacles, and adventure challenges.

22. Full Rope Course (30+ Activities) Complete a series of over 30
    rope-based adventure challenges.

23. Paint Ball Enjoy a team-based tactical game in an exciting outdoor
    battlefield environment.

24. Zip Zap Zoom A multi-adventure package combining several aerial
    attractions.

25. Sky Tour Combo A bundled experience featuring multiple signature
    adventure activities.

26. Full Park Access (42+ Activities) An all-access pass allowing
    visitors to enjoy more than 42 adventure activities.
